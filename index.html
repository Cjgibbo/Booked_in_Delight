Briefing — 003

The Bookmark Ceremony

Forget algorithms. Forget Goodreads suggestions from 2019. Here, book selection happens the way it should — a blind draw. Everyone puts a pick in. A bookmark gets pulled.

Think Love Island, but instead of choosing someone who ghosted their ex, you're choosing a novel that might keep you up until 3 AM on a Tuesday.

Your bookmark could be next. Accept it.

Briefing — 005

What Actually Happens Here

People showed up expecting small talk. What they got was community. Real conversations — the kind where someone says something funny, then someone says something deep, and then you realize you've been there an hour longer than planned.

Acquaintances walked in. Actual friends walked out.

That's not a marketing line. That's what happened.

“Let me tell you — the funny, deep conversations that happen are not to be missed. We are technically the talk of the town. So come join.”

Brandi — The Librarian, Delight Public Library

When the librarian personally vouch­es for it, you don't ask follow-up questions. You show up.

The Part Nobody Warns You About

You'll stay up too late. Not because you have to — because the book won't let you stop. Every week someone falls in love with a book they didn't pick, didn't expect, didn't see coming. That's the whole point.

And the funniest moment in the club's history?

You wouldn't believe it if I told you. 🫣

Briefing — 008

This Is Your Elimination Round

On The Challenge, you don't get to sit on the couch and watch. You're either in the arena or you're on a flight home.

This is the same thing. Except the arena is air-conditioned, the competition is a paperback, and the only elimination is self-imposed regret when you hear about the conversation you missed.

Nobody gets voted off. But you do have to vote yourself in.

So, Jamie.
Are you in?

Weekly meetings. One book at a time. Deep conversations that are apparently the talk of the town. Friendships that started as acquaintances. And at least one moment per month that nobody would believe.

You asked if I was joining. I'm asking you back.

Bring a book pick. Bring a baked good. Show up.
