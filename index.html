How It Works

Simple by Design

1
Read the book. At your pace. On your couch. In the bath. We don't judge.
2
Show up the last Monday of the month. 6 PM. The library. You know where it is.
3
Talk about it. The parts you loved. The parts you didn't. The character you'd fight.
4
Suggest a book for next month. One gets drawn from the hat. That's the next read.
